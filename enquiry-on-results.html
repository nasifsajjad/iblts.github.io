Enquiry on Results

If you believe there has been an error in your IBLTS results, you can request an enquiry.

1
Complete the Enquiry on Results form within six weeks of your test date.
2
Pay the enquiry fee (refundable if your score changes).
3
Submit the form to your test center or via the IBLTS website.
4
Wait for the re-evaluation process, which may take up to 21 days.

For more details, please contact your test center or IBLTS support.
