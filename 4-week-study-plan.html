4-Week Study Plan

This 4-week study plan is designed to help you prepare effectively for the IBLTS test. Each week focuses on different aspects of the test to ensure comprehensive preparation.

Week Listening Reading Writing Speaking Notes
1 Practice with sample audio clips Read short passages Learn writing task formats Practice speaking on common topics Focus on understanding the test structure
2 Work on note-taking while listening Practice with longer passages Write practice essays Practice with a partner or tutor Start timing your practice sessions
3 Take a full listening practice test Focus on identifying key information Get feedback on your essays Record and review your speaking Identify weak areas to improve
4 Review listening strategies Practice skimming and scanning Write under timed conditions Simulate the speaking test Take a full practice test
