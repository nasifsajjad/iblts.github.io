Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IBLTS tests.

What is IBLTS?
IBLTS is the International Bangla Language Testing System, a standardized test to assess Bangla proficiency.
Who can take the test?
Anyone needing to prove their Bangla skills for education, work, or migration.
