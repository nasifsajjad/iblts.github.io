Preparation Tips by Section

Listening

  • Practice listening to different Bangla accents
  • Develop note-taking skills
  • Focus on understanding main ideas and specific details
  • Practice following multi-step instructions

Reading

  • Expand your academic vocabulary
  • Practice skimming and scanning techniques
  • Work on understanding writer's opinions and attitudes
  • Practice time management

Writing

  • Practice organizing ideas logically
  • Work on varying sentence structures
  • Learn to analyze visual information
  • Develop essay planning techniques

Speaking

  • Practice speaking on a variety of topics
  • Record yourself and analyze your speech
  • Work on pronunciation and fluency
  • Practice organizing extended speaking turns
