Result Validity

IBLTS results are valid for a specific period, depending on the purpose of the test.

Validity Period

IBLTS results are generally valid for two years from the test date. After this period, you may need to retake the test to prove your current proficiency.

Using Your Results

Results can be used for applications to universities, employers, or immigration authorities within the validity period. Always check the specific requirements of the organization you are applying to.
