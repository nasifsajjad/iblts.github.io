8-Week Study Plan

This 8-week study plan provides a comprehensive approach to preparing for the IBLTS test, allowing for a gradual buildup of skills and knowledge.

Week Listening Reading Writing Speaking Notes
1 Understand the listening test format Learn about the reading test structure Familiarize with writing tasks Practice introducing yourself Focus on test overview
2 Practice with short dialogues Read simple passages Study grammar rules Practice describing pictures Build basic skills
3 Work on identifying main ideas Practice skimming techniques Write short paragraphs Practice answering common questions Improve comprehension
4 Take a practice listening test Focus on vocabulary building Learn essay structures Record and review your speech Mid-point assessment
5 Practice note-taking Read longer articles Write full essays Practice with a partner Enhance writing and speaking
6 Focus on different accents Practice with academic texts Get feedback on essays Simulate speaking test conditions Refine skills
7 Review listening strategies Take a full reading practice test Practice under timed conditions Work on fluency and coherence Final preparations
8 Take a full practice test Review weak areas Revise key writing points Practice relaxation techniques Ensure readiness for test day
