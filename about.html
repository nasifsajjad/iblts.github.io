About IBLTS

The International Bangla Language Testing System (IBLTS) is the world's most popular Bangla language proficiency test for higher education and global migration. IBLTS is trusted by governments, employers, and educational institutions around the world.

Our Mission

To provide a fair, accurate, and reliable assessment of Bangla language proficiency, helping individuals achieve their academic and professional goals while supporting organizations in making informed decisions.

Our History

Founded in 2025, IBLTS was developed by leading language assessment experts to address the growing need for a standardized Bangla proficiency test. We've continuously evolved our testing methods while maintaining the highest standards of assessment quality.

Our Team

IBLTS is developed and administered by a global team of language assessment specialists, educators, and technology experts committed to excellence in language testing.
